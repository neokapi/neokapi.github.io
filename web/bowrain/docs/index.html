Bowrain

The team platform for keeping content on brand and in every language: shared, versioned governance of brand voice, terminology, and translation memory — that learns from every correction — plus collaborative editing, connectors to the systems your content already lives in, and automation. It governs the content kapi parses and edits — the persistent, multi-user layer a team needs.
